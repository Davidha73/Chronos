Clock Face Guide

Sleep Window (Purple Bar) The thick purple arc on the outer rim represents your scheduled sleep time. Optimal recovery happens here.
Workout Window (Orange Bar) The orange arc on the second rim represents your training session (only shown on Training Days). Helps anchor pre- and post-workout supplement timing.
Meal Anchors (Green Diamonds) Represent Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner. These act as circadian anchors to automatically align supplement intake.
Clash Warnings (Red Triangles) Alerts you to circadian or chemical conflicts, such as taking caffeine near bedtime, vitamin D3 too late, or timing clashes between supplements.
Supplement Indicators (Colored Circles) Circles mark scheduled supplements, situated on concentric category tracks:
• Muscle (Light Blue): Creatine, Whey, Casein, EAAs
• Performance (Purple): Caffeine, Beta-Alanine, Citrulline, Alpha-GPC, Tyrosine, Electrolytes
• Health (Green): Omega-3, Vit D3+K2, Multivitamin, Ashwagandha, Turmeric, T-Booster
• Sleep (Indigo): ZMA, Magnesium, Theanine, Melatonin, Apigenin
Dimmed Indicators (Absorption Conflicts) When a supplement's dot is faded, it indicates a minor absorption issue (e.g., taking Magnesium/ZMA too close to calcium or a heavy dinner).
